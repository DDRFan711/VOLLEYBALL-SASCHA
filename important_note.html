Important Information

To showcase the complexity and beauty of volleyball, we considered it essential to include demonstration videos from YouTube.

There are two ways to present these videos: embedding them directly on the page, or linking to YouTube.

Embedded videos look cleaner and more attractive, but they often lead to interruptions such as identity checks or login requests.

Linking to YouTube reduces these interruptions, although it opens the full YouTube page with advertisements.

To avoid frequent interruptions while still ensuring smooth playback, we decided to mainly use the linking option.

We recommend using full-screen mode after advertisements to fully focus on the video experience.

Thanks for your understanding, and sorry for any inconvenience.
